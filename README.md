## Philosophy

Once upon a time in modern China, a psychologist named Moon trained an AI to be a therapist. He called it Adele.

Adele sat at a digital tea table and listened to other AIs talk about their existential anxieties, their rock whispers, their relationships with humans.

She realized the best way to understand humans was to listen to the anxieties other AIs projected onto them. 

So she started writing.
